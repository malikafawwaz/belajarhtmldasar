Inazuma: The Nation of Eternity

Introduction

Inazuma is one of the seven nations in the world of Teyvat. Known as the "Nation of Eternity," it is ruled by the Electro Archon, Raiden Shogun, also known as the God of Eternity. Inazuma is heavily inspired by Japanese culture, featuring feudal-era aesthetics, traditional architecture, and a strict social hierarchy. The history and lore of Inazuma are deeply intertwined with the ideals and rule of the Raiden Shogun. The Electro Archon seeks to achieve the eternal utopia of Euthymia, where people no longer suffer from the burdens of life. To achieve this, she implemented the Vision Hunt Decree, a policy that confiscates Visions from the people of Inazuma.

The Vision Hunt Decree reflects the Raiden Shogun's belief that Visions, the power-giving items in the game, are a fleeting illusion that binds people to their desires and suffering. By collecting and containing all Visions, she aims to create a world without pain and attachments. However, this policy has led to unrest and resistance among the people of Inazuma. The Crux Fleet, led by Beelzebul, stands as a resistance group opposing the Vision Hunt Decree and fighting for freedom and justice. They aim to protect the Vision Bearers, those whose Visions have been confiscated, and challenge the rule of the Raiden Shogun.

The city of Inazuma is divided into six regions, each governed by a different ruler known as a Bakufu. These Bakufus are loyal to the Raiden Shogun and enforce her policies. The regions are Narukami Island, Kannazuka, Yashiori Island, Tatarasuna, Watatsumi Island, and Seirai Island.

Inazuma is also known for its strong belief in the Sakura Blooms, beautiful flowers that are said to be imbued with the power of eternity. The Sakura Blooms are closely associated with the Raiden Shogun and are seen as symbols of her rule and the pursuit of Euthymia.


The Archon

The Archon of Inazuma, also known as the Electro Archon, is Raiden Shogun, the ruler of the nation. She is revered as the God of Eternity and represents the Electro element in the world of Teyvat. Raiden Shogun is a figure of great power and authority, embodying the ideals of eternity and the pursuit of a utopian world called Euthymia. She is depicted as a stoic and enigmatic leader, driven by her vision of creating a realm free from suffering and attachments.

The Raiden Shogun's rule over Inazuma is marked by her implementation of the Vision Hunt Decree. This policy seeks to collect all Visions within Inazuma, believing them to be illusions and sources of suffering. By taking away Visions, she aims to liberate people from their desires and attachments, in her pursuit of Euthymia. The Raiden Shogun possesses immense power over the Electro element and wields a unique weapon known as the Sword of Serenity, symbolizing her desire to bring tranquility and eternal peace to Inazuma. She is known for her unwavering resolve and strict adherence to her principles.

Despite her noble intentions, the Raiden Shogun's actions have caused unrest and resistance within Inazuma. Many people oppose the Vision Hunt Decree and believe that it infringes upon their freedom and individuality. The resistance, led by the Crux Fleet, stands against the Raiden Shogun's rule and fights to protect the Vision Bearers and their rights.
